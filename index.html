Event Overview

IEEE TECH FUTURES 2024

Welcome to IEEE Tech Futures 2024, a premier event dedicated to exploring the future of technology and innovation.

Join us for an exciting day of insightful talks, networking opportunities, and hands-on workshops.

This event is designed for students, professionals, and enthusiasts who are passionate about technology and its impact on the world.

Details

Title: IEEE Tech Futures 2024

Date: September 15, 2024

Venue: Rajagiri School of Engineering and Technology, Kochi, Kerala

Time: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM IST

Schedule

9:00 AM - 10:00 AM: Registration and Welcome Breakfast

10:00 AM - 11:00 AM: Opening Keynote: "The Future of AI" by Dr. Ayesha Khan

11:00 AM - 12:00 PM: Panel Discussion: "Sustainable Technology for the Future"

12:00 PM - 1:00 PM: Networking Lunch

1:00 PM - 2:30 PM: Workshop: "Building with Blockchain" led by Rajesh Menon

2:30 PM - 3:00 PM: Coffee Break

3:00 PM - 4:00 PM: Tech Talk: "Cybersecurity Trends" by Neha Gupta

4:00 PM - 5:30 PM: Interactive Session: "Innovative IoT Solutions" by Anil Kumar

5:30 PM - 6:00 PM: Closing Remarks and Award Ceremony
